About me

Hi! My name is Ana Bombín Ordás. I am a fourth-year student at Parsons School of Design in New York, focusing my studies on strategic design and management (BBA) and double minoring in communication design and creative coding. I love crafting immersive digital experiences using tools like Figma and code.

With experience in creative agencies, and start-ups as a UX designer, I've developed a fascination for digital, interactive art. Which I hope to use as a means to inspire young women to dive into creative technology. I'm excited to continue exploring multi-sensory expression through interactive and generative systems, creating educational materials, and nurturing the next generation of creative minds and yes, girls who code like me.

Through my studies, I have explored many mediums and areas of interest. Ranging from fine art to digital and strategic design. This has helped me create a diversified portfolio of projects. Though this is a more curated version of my portfolio if you wish to explore further, head on over to my full portfolio site: full portfolio.
