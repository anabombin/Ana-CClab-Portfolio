Consentful Interface

Process

Description

With this sketch, I was kind of stumped at the beginning. I had any ideas but none seemed to work out either because p5 was too slow to process, and VSCode would glitch. Eventually, I became so frustrated that I felt like I was seeing double on my code and could no longer concentrate. From there, I came up with this idea. I wanted my experimental camera to represent how tired and out of focus eyes get the more time you spend looking at a screen.

Process

I started by creating a simple grid that followed the hours. So, it started at midnight with just one square, and as the hours go by, the number of rows and columns would align with the hours to make the grid smaller until midnight. Then, I incorporated the video, by replacing squares with the current video of the camera. Finally, to make it more realistic to myself and the hours I spend on the screen, I changed it to start at 8AM, with one grid, and start climbing from there. This way, it aligns with my own schedule and time spend on the computer throughout the day.
